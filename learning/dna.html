DNA是什么？
What is DNA? (小学三年级双语科普)

1. DNA的基本介绍 / What is DNA?

DNA是我们身体里的神奇小分子。它的全名叫脱氧核糖核酸。DNA就像一本秘密的说明书，告诉我们的身体怎么长，长什么样。

DNA is a special part inside our body. Its full name is Deoxyribonucleic Acid. DNA is like a secret book. It tells our body how to grow and what we look like.

2. DNA长什么样？ / What does DNA look like?

DNA看起来像一个扭扭的梯子，我们叫它“双螺旋”。这个梯子有两边，中间有很多“台阶”，这些台阶是用四种特殊的“小块”做的，这些小块就是A、T、G、C。

DNA looks like a twisted ladder. We call it a "double helix." The ladder has two sides and many "steps." The steps are made of four special pieces. They are called A, T, G, and C.

3. DNA有什么用？ / What does DNA do?

  • DNA让我们有黑头发、棕眼睛或者高个子。
  • 它告诉我们的身体怎么做蛋白质。蛋白质是让我们健康、强壮的“工人”。
  • DNA也决定我们和家人很像，因为我们从爸爸妈妈那里得到DNA。
  • DNA gives us black hair, brown eyes, or makes us tall.
  • It tells our body how to make proteins. Proteins help us be healthy and strong.
  • We look like our family because we get DNA from our mom and dad.

4. DNA是怎么传下去的？ / How is DNA passed on?

我们每个人的DNA都是从爸爸妈妈那里各来一半，所以我们有点像爸爸，也有点像妈妈。动物、小草、花朵都有自己的DNA。正因为有了DNA，小猫才会生小猫，小狗才会生小狗。

Each of us gets half of our DNA from mom and half from dad. This is why we look a bit like both. Animals, grass, and flowers also have their own DNA. Because of DNA, cats have kittens and dogs have puppies.

5. DNA和健康 / DNA and Health

有时候，DNA会发生一些小变化，科学家叫它“突变”。有些突变会让人生病，比如有的人天生容易近视，有的人容易过敏。医生可以检查DNA，帮我们更早发现疾病。

Sometimes, DNA can change a little. Scientists call this a "mutation." Some mutations can make us sick. For example, some people are born with poor eyesight, or may get allergies easily. Doctors can look at our DNA to help find sickness early.

6. DNA在生活中的应用 / How do we use DNA in real life?

  • 科学家用DNA找出犯罪嫌疑人。
  • 医生用DNA帮忙找病因。
  • 有些农民用DNA让水果变得更大更甜。
  • Scientists use DNA to find people who did bad things.
  • Doctors use DNA to help find out why people are sick.
  • Some farmers use DNA to make fruits bigger and sweeter.

7. DNA的趣味知识 / Fun Facts about DNA

  • 每个人的DNA都不一样，除了同卵双胞胎！
  • 如果把你身体里的DNA拉直，可以绕地球几百万圈！
  • 世界上所有的生物都有DNA。
  • Everyone’s DNA is different, except for identical twins!
  • If you stretch out all the DNA in your body, it could go around the Earth millions of times!
  • All living things in the world have DNA.

8. 小结 / Summary

DNA是生命的秘密密码。它让我们和家人、朋友和世界上的动物、植物都有不同的样子。DNA很神奇，科学家还在不断探索它的奥秘。保护好我们的DNA，健康成长吧！

DNA is the secret code of life. It makes us and our family, friends, animals, and plants all look different. DNA is amazing, and scientists are still learning more about it. Let’s take care of our DNA and grow up healthy!
